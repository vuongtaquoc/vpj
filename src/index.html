About Us

Take your chance to participate in Viet Startup Contest (VSC) 2019!

The program welcomes all innovative ideas to solve critical problems of Vietnam and to build a sustainable future for the country. VSC 2019 is organized by VANJ (Vietnamese Academic Network in Japan), VPJ (Vietnamese Professionals in Japan) and Vingroup Japan – a Japanese subsidiary of Vingroup in Vietnam.

Participate VSC 2019 to get a chance to win the ﬁrst prize of 1,000,000 JPY! Furthermore, attendants will receive guidance and advice from experienced advisors and judges to develop and expand their ideas!

PARTICIPANTS QUALIFICATIONS

VSC welcomes all business ideas coming from individuals and groups of all ages and nationalities, residing inside or outside Japan. However, we encourage ideas contributing to the development of Vietnam, and it will be one of the important judging criteria.

Applicants outside of Japan can participate in the Monthly hearing online. However, if selected for the Final Contest, they must come to Japan to attend the event. VSC organizer does not cover travel expenses to Japan.
